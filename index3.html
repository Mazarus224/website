History

Hello there! I'm Malik Robinson, and I'd like to take you on a journey through the chapters of my life. I spent my formative years in the picturesque state of Colorado, where I discovered my love for music. The saxophone became my faithful companion, providing a creative outlet and a source of joy. The ability to express myself through music has been a defining aspect of my identity, shaping my experiences and fostering a deep appreciation for the arts. After completing my education at Boulder High School, I embarked on a path that took me to unexpected places. Eager for new challenges and experiences, I made the bold decision to join the military. This period of my life was marked by discipline, camaraderie, and the opportunity to serve my country. As part of this journey, I found myself living in the vibrant and culturally rich landscapes of England, an experience that broadened my perspectives and introduced me to diverse ways of life. Following my military service, I set my sights on new horizons. The journey led me to the welcoming community of Spokane, a city nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. It was here that I decided to continue my education, and fate guided me to North Idaho College. At North Idaho College, I found a home for my academic pursuits, diving into the world of computer science. The transition from military life to the vibrant college atmosphere brought its own set of challenges, but it also opened doors to a wealth of opportunities. I embraced the dynamic curriculum, fueled by a passion for technology and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving field of computer science. Throughout this journey, my saxophone remained a constant companion, providing solace and inspiration. Whether in the military barracks, the rolling hills of England, or the bustling campus of North Idaho College, the saxophone has been a thread weaving through the tapestry of my experiences. As I reflect on the chapters behind me, I am grateful for the diverse experiences that have shaped my journey. The road from Colorado to North Idaho College has been filled with twists and turns, challenges and triumphs, and I eagerly anticipate the adventures that lie ahead. Thank you for joining me on this retrospective exploration, and feel free to reach out if you share a passion for music, military service, or the exciting world of computer science.
